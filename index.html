Emotional support platform for nurses

Nurse Platform

An app entirely designed for nurses which will allow them to share their knowledge, experience, and thoughts in a confidential manner, and facilitate their communication. Moreover, nurses will be able to express their emotions and opinions in a supportive and understanding environment.

The app will contain feedback from patients which is used to help nurses develop a better connection with their patients, improve their work skills, and motivate them in their future work. As a result nurses will feel more connected, less emotionally overwhelmed and ultimately more satisfied and productive.

Features

  • Feed

    The feed will display posts made by the nurses. Nurses can leave comments and like the posts. The posts will be arranged in recent, popular, and urgent categories as well as an option to change from all posts or specific departments.

  • Post

    Nurses can share their concerns, issues and emotions, as posts with the option of being anonymous. They can receive responses from other nurses advice, tips and comforting messages.

  • Personal Activity

    The personal activity tab will provide the nurses with notifications about the likes and comments on their posts. They can also view previous posts that they have made and the posts from other nurses that are beneficial to them that they have chosen to 'follow'.

  • Private Messaging

    In the private messenger nurses can send direct messages to specific nurses as well as create groups.

  • Profile

    In the profile tab, nurses can view their personal information, a list of the patients they are associated with and their personal feedback.
